This code defines a set of data types representing playing cards, such as suits, ranks, and cards. It also includes functions for reading card inputs and determining the type of hand based on the cards provided. The `nameHand` function categorizes the hand of cards as straight flush, four of a kind, full house, flush, straight, three of a kind, two pair, one pair, or high card. The `testHands` list contains various combinations of cards to test the `nameHand` function, and the `main` function outputs the category of hand for each test case.
