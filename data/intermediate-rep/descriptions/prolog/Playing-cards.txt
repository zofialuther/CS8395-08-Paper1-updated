This code defines a module for representing and manipulating a deck of playing cards. A card is represented as a term "card(Pip, Suit)" and a deck is represented internally as a list of cards. The module provides predicates for creating a new deck, shuffling the deck, dealing a card from the deck, and printing the deck.
